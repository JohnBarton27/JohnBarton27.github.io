Suntree UMC Online

Project Timeframe

March 2020 - October 2023

Client

Suntree United Methodist Church

About This Project

When the COVID-19 pandemic hit in March 2020, many local organizations were forced to go remote. Suntree UMC was no exception - Blue Triangle provided equipment & resoruces necessary to help pre-record worship services so that the congregation could stay at home. Today, Blue Triangle continues to help during SUMC weekly live streams and with other production needs.
