Hispanic Heritage Month 2014

Project Timeframe

August - September 2014

Client

University of Florida - Hispanic Student Association (HSA)

About This Project

UF's Hispanic Student Association trusted Blue Triangle to deliver when they needed a promotional campaign for their annual Hispanic Heritage Month kickoff event. We produced 2 short promos and a full "welcome video" for the event, all of which were incredibly well-received by HSA. We had our team work closely with the leadership of HSA, who wrote the spoken word and choreographed the dance performance shown in the videos, to bring their vision to life. We sent two team members to the kickoff event to ensure the video launched successfully at their grand opening, which it did.
